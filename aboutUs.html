T.U.L.O.N.G.

Tactical Unit Locator & Operations Network Grid (T.U.L.O.N.G.) was developed to revolutionize community-level emergency response within Cebu City.

Traditional dispatch systems often struggle with the "last mile"—the critical gap between receiving an alarm and the arrival of responders in dense urban environments. Our grid provides real-time tactical data to bridge this gap, ensuring that help arrives exactly where it is needed, without delay.

This system is maintained by the Tinago Fire Volunteers and is dedicated to the safety of our local community through technical innovation and volunteer coordination.
